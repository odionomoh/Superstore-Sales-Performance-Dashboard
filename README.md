##  Project Overview
This project analyzes the Global Superstore dataset to uncover insights into sales performance, profitability, and market trends.

The objective is to build an interactive dashboard that transforms raw data into actionable business insights.

##  Analysis & Key Insights

### 💰 Sales Performance
- Total Sales: **$12.6M+**
- Technology leads with **$4.7M+**
- Sales peaked around **2014** before declining
- Some products generate high sales but low profit  
- Loss orders exist and require attention  


---

### 📈 Profitability
- Total Profit: **$1.5M+**
- Technology is most profitable  
- Furniture shows lower profit despite high sales  

---

### ⚖️ Profit vs Loss
- **76% Profit**
- **24% Loss**

Loss-making transactions significantly impact performance.

---

### 🎯 Discount Impact
- Higher discounts reduce profit  
- Furniture & Office Supplies most affected  
- Technology is more resilient  

---

### 🌍 Market Performance
- APAC leads in sales and profit  
- EU and US follow  
- Canada is lowest  

---

### 🗺️ Regional Distribution
- Central region leads (**$2.8M+**)  
- South and North follow  
- Lower regions show growth potential  

---

### 🚚 Shipping Insights
- Standard Class:
  - Highest sales  
  - Longest delivery (5 days)

Customers prioritize cost over speed.

---

### 🏆 Product Performance
- Top products dominated by Technology  
- Bottom products show:
  - Low demand  
  - Low profitability  

---

##  Business Recommendations
- Optimize discount strategies  
- Focus on APAC market expansion  
- Improve low-performing regions  
- Enhance Standard Class efficiency  
- Review or discontinue weak products
